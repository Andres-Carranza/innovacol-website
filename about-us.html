Mission Statement



InnovaCol aims to bridge the technological gap between latinamerica and the rest of the world through education. Our goal is to encourage innovation in the region by teaching students how to harness the powers of technology.

Meet Our Team

Andres Carranza

Founder and President

Andres Carranza is a high school junior from Bogota, Colombia. He is very passionate about Machine Learning, Computer Science, and Physics. Andres has over 5 years of programming experience, has had the opportunity to carry out Machine Learning research at NASA's Ames Research Center, and won the 2020 Colombian Computational Olympiad. Additionally, he will be participating in this years Research Science Institute, an intensive research program hosted by the Massachussets Institute of Technology, where he will carry out computational astrophysics research at the Harvard-Smithsonian Center for Astrophysics. Andres hopes to share all his experience with latinamerican students through InnovaCol, with the long term goal of shifting the economy of Colombia and other latinamerican countries to become more innovative and technology driven.
