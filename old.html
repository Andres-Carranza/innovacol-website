InnovaCol at a Glance

InnovaCol is a nonprofit founded by passionate students talented in STEM. Our organizations strives to improve the quality of STEM education in Colombia and other latinamerican countries. We hope to provide students with the knowledge and opportunities that they will need to lead successful careers and contribute to innovation in latinamerica. InnovaCol offers courses on Python and Machine Learning, in which students can learn the basics of the emerging fields of computer science and artificial intelligence. All of our courses are free of cost! Our mission is to reach as many latinamerican youth as possible and offer them the resources that they need.
