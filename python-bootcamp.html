Course Information

Schedule:

Our Python classes will take place from Wednesday June 2nd to Wednesday June 30th. Classes will be held weekly on Wednesdays and Saturdays. Each class will take place on Zoom from 5pm-7pm Colombian Time (UTC-5).

Our class is open to beginners of all ages! No previous programming experience is necessary. However, we do require that all students have a proficient level of english.

Cost:
